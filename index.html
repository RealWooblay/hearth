FAQ

Real questions, real answers.

What happens if my Wi-Fi goes down?

HEARTH keeps working. Modules talk to Core over a local Thread mesh, not your router. The only thing you'll lose is access to the internet from your modules — the AI itself runs entirely on Core.

Can I add modules later?

Yes. Every module pairs in under 30 seconds. Founders get 20% off every future module, permanently.

Is my data really private?

It really is. There's no cloud account because there's no cloud. You can verify it: an optional packet-capture mode shows you every byte Core sends out, and it's none.

When will I get mine?

Founders ship Q4 2026 in numbered order of reservation. Standard reservations ship Q1–Q2 2027. We'll lock your slot at deposit.

Do I need a subscription?

The hub and most modules — no subscription, ever. Software updates are free for life. Three modules (Pantry Sentinel, Watcher, Dietician) include an optional Intelligence layer at $12–$20/mo that powers the data-heavy features. The hardware itself works without it; you just lose the longitudinal analysis.

What about my Alexa / Google Home / HomeKit stuff?

HEARTH speaks Matter and Thread natively, so it'll inherit most of what you've already got. Most beta users retire their previous hubs within the first month.

What's the refund policy?

Your $99 deposit is fully refundable until the moment your unit ships. After delivery, you have 30 days to return the hardware for a full refund.
